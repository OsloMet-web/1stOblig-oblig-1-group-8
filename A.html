GROUP WORK

Benefits of working in a group

It can be easier to motivate yourself for the course when working in small groups. To know that the work you put in affects everyone in the group can increase quality and productivity because you feel a responsibility. The influence of other people could inspire by their knowledge and passion of the course. Having motivated group members spreads positivity through the group. Furthermore, group work can result in long lasting friendships. Having a healthy social life is important both to perform in the course, but also to live a happy and fulfilling life in general.
